Frequently Asked Questions

  • No prep work is required, but feel free to take a look at the Open GSA site and wiki to get a jump start on your project.

  • The National Archives Building is located between Seventh and Ninth Streets, NW, with an entrance to the Innovation Hub on Pennsylvania Avenue.

    Subway: In Washington, DC, take Metrorail's Yellow or Green lines to the Archives/Navy Memorial station. The Archives/Navy Memorial stop is across Pennsylvania Avenue from the Archives building.

    Bus: In Washington, DC, Metrobuses 30, 32, 34, 36, 53, A42, A46, A48, P1, P2, P4, P17, P19, and W13 stop at the National Archives on Pennsylvania Avenue.

    For more specific information on routes, fares, and timetables for Metrobus and Metrorail, please see the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority or telephone Metro at 202-637-7000.

    Parking: No parking at the building is available. Several commercial parking lots are located nearby and metered curb parking may be available on nearby streets.

  • No, you don't need to form a team before attending. We'll set aside time in the morning to help with team formation.

  • Many coffee shops and restaurants are located near the Innovation Hub, several are right across the street. Food is allowed in the Innovation Hub, so feel free to bring items with you or run out to lunch.

  • If you are a designer or project manager, we still need your skills! There are many ways to contribute to a team, even if you're not a developer. Come out and show us what you got.
